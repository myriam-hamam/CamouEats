About CampuEats

Your dedicated food delivery service for Misr International University (MIU) students.

Our Mission

To provide MIU students with convenient, fast, and diverse food delivery options, connecting them to both on-campus food courts and popular local restaurants, right to their university building.

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted food delivery platform for university campuses across Egypt, enhancing student life through seamless access to quality meals.

Our Story

CampuEats was born out of a simple idea: students at MIU deserve easy access to great food, whether they're studying late in the library, rushing between classes, or just relaxing in their dorms. We noticed the gap between campus dining and external options, and decided to bridge it. Our team, comprised of passionate MIU students, is committed to making your campus life more delicious and convenient.

We've partnered with both the beloved MIU food court vendors and a selection of top-rated restaurants outside the university to bring you a wide variety of choices. Our goal is to save you time and effort, so you can focus on what truly matters: your studies and campus experience.
